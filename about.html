About mAIques Lab

Led by Dr. Oscar Maiques, the mAIques Lab investigates how extracellular matrix (ECM) structure and alignment influence cancer initiation, progression, and therapy response.

We integrate artificial intelligence with spatial omics, 3D organotypic models, and patient-derived data to uncover fundamental principles of tumor biology and translate them into diagnostic and therapeutic innovations.
