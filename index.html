Welcome to mAIques Lab

Decoding the Matrix of Cancer with AI, Spatial Biology, and Next-Generation 3D Models.

About

Led by Dr. Oscar Maiques, the mAIques Lab investigates how extracellular matrix (ECM) structure and alignment influence cancer initiation, progression, and therapy response. We integrate artificial intelligence with spatial omics, 3D organotypic models, and patient-derived data to uncover fundamental principles of tumor biology and translate them into diagnostic and therapeutic innovations.

Research

  • Early Detection of Melanoma, Pancreatic, and Breast Cancer
  • ECM Alignment, Tissue Architecture & Mechanobiology
  • Spatial Transcriptomics, Imaging Mass Cytometry & Multiplexed Proteomics
  • 3D Human Skin Equivalents, Organoids & Bioprinting
