Welcome to mAIques Lab

Decoding the Matrix of Cancer with AI and Spatial Biology

About

mAIques Lab is led by Dr. Oscar Maiques and investigates how extracellular matrix (ECM) structure and alignment influence cancer initiation, progression, and therapy response. We integrate AI with spatial omics and 3D models to uncover fundamental principles of tumor biology and translational applications.

Research

  • Early Detection of Melanoma, Pancreatic, and Breast Cancer
  • ECM Alignment & Mechanobiology
  • Spatial Transcriptomics and Imaging Mass Cytometry
  • Organoids, Human Skin Equivalents & 3D Modelling
